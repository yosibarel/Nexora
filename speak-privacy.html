Information We Collect

Speak View may access audio files, voice messages, and related shared content that you choose to send to the app in order to provide its core features.

Speak View does not collect personal information such as your name, address, or payment details unless explicitly stated inside the app.

The app uses shared audio content only to provide transcription, language detection, on-device processing, floating display, and related app functionality.

Language preferences selected in the app may be stored locally on your device so the app can remember your settings.

Data Processing

Speak View is designed to process supported audio content locally on your device for transcription features.

If future versions of the app introduce optional cloud-based features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before such features are used.

Data Sharing

Speak View does not sell personal data. Speak View does not share your personal information with third parties except when necessary to comply with legal obligations or protect the app and its users.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information processed by the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Audio content processed by Speak View is kept only as needed for the app’s functionality on your device. Users can remove app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

Permissions

Speak View may request access to features such as overlay display and access to shared audio content in order to provide its main functionality.

These permissions are used only for app features requested by the user.

Children’s Privacy
